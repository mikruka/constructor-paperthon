What is Constructor Datathon?

It's an intensive 4-day datathon designed to equip you with the skills to conduct high-quality Systematic Literature Reviews and Meta-Analysis using cutting-edge tools.

üîç What is an SLR and why does it matter?

A Systematic Literature Review (SLR) is a powerful research method used to identify, evaluate, and synthesize all available studies relevant to a specific research question. Unlike traditional literature reviews, an SLR follows a structured, transparent, and reproducible process ‚Äî making it the gold standard in evidence-based research.

Why is it essential?

  • To map the current state of knowledge in any domain.
  • To identify research gaps and trends.
  • To provide credible, data-driven insights for decision-makers, scholars, and innovators.

üìä What is Meta-Analysis and why does it matter?

Meta-analysis is a statistical technique used to quantitatively combine the results of studies identified in your SLR.

Meta-Analysis allows you to:

  • Derive robust, generalizable conclusions.
  • Reveal hidden patterns or contradictions.
  • Resolve contradictions in existing research.
  • Increase the statistical power of your findings.

üí° Please note: For the Meta-Analysis stage, including vote counting methods, we will provide ready-to-use Python scripts and guidance separately, so you can directly apply them to the outputs of your SLR.

üíª The Constructor Platform: Your Research Game-Changer

The Constructor Platform serves as a game-changer tool for performing SLR and Meta-Analysis using integrated tools, including the construction of Knowledge Models. Participants will be granted exclusive access to the Constructor Platform.

With the Platform, you'll be able to:

  • Interact with scientific papers using LLMs to extract key claims, evidence, and methods.
  • Build structured, explainable Knowledge Models from your findings.
  • Test hypothetical theses ‚Äî the system will automatically check if a given claim is supported or unaddressed in the literature.
  • Enhance your review process through semantic search.
  • Easily organize information in a machine-readable, exportable format.

Whether you're a student or an early-career researcher, mastering SLRs is a gateway to impactful research and high-quality publications.
